My career in technology started early while working in my family’s design agency, where I gained hands-on experience in branding, design, web development, multimedia production, and copywriting. That early exposure sparked a long-term passion for technology and digital problem solving.

Over the past 15 years, I’ve worked as a freelance web developer and technology consultant, helping individuals and businesses build, improve, and manage their digital presence. Through this work, I developed a strong technical foundation while learning how technology intersects with business needs and user experience.

I later earned my degree in Information Technology and Administrative Management with a Cybersecurity specialization from Central Washington University, where I deepened my focus on security, infrastructure, and protecting modern systems.

Today I’m focused on continuing to grow in cybersecurity and working toward a role as a Security Operations Center (SOC) Analyst. I’m particularly interested in threat monitoring, incident response, and defensive security strategies, and I continue to study and build skills in these areas.

When I’m not working with technology, you’ll usually find me learning about new cybersecurity tools, experimenting with development projects, or playing music.
